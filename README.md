You're lost in the woods at night. A house appears. An old lady lets you in and offers you tea.
You say yes because what else are you going to do.
She goes to get it. She doesn't come back. The house is different now.
Find a way to leave!

Controls: 
Arrows - move forward,back,up, and down
Spacebar- Interact

Obstacles:
Room 1 - Maze
Navigate through the maze. Find the key, find the door.
Room 2 - Dark Room
Collect all the items in the dark while avoiding obstacles. One of the items is a tomato.
Room 3 - Ghost
There's a ghost, and it's chasing you. Grab the key and go.
GOOD LUCK
